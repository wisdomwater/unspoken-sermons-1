# George MacDonald — *Unspoken Sermons* Devotional Adaptation

This repository contains a devotional adaptation of **George MacDonald's *Unspoken Sermons***, written to make one of Christianity's greatest spiritual classics accessible to modern readers while remaining faithful to MacDonald's theology, voice, and pastoral heart.

Rather than producing a paraphrase or commentary, this project seeks to express each sermon as though **George MacDonald were speaking directly to today's reader** in clear, contemporary English.

## Purpose

MacDonald's writings have influenced generations of Christian thinkers, including C. S. Lewis, G. K. Chesterton, and Madeleine L'Engle. Yet many readers find his nineteenth-century prose difficult to navigate.

The goal of this project is to preserve the depth of his thought while removing the barriers of Victorian language, creating a devotional experience that is easier to read without sacrificing theological richness.

## Editorial Philosophy

Every devotional follows these principles:

* Faithful to MacDonald's theology and line of reasoning
* Written in warm, modern contemplative English
* Focused on revealing the character of the Father through Jesus Christ
* Preserves MacDonald's pastoral and hopeful voice
* Invites contemplation rather than argument
* Ends with a brief Breath Prayer for meditation

This is **not** intended to replace *Unspoken Sermons*, but to serve as a companion that helps readers engage more deeply with MacDonald's vision of God.

## Structure

Each devotional contains:

* A title
* A short aphorism
* Four to five devotional reflections
* A Breath Prayer

The movement of each reading follows MacDonald's own pattern:

> Scripture → Revelation of God's Character → Reflection → Invitation → Hope

## Major Themes

Throughout the series, several themes appear again and again:

* The Fatherhood of God
* Christ as the perfect revelation of the Father
* God's absolute goodness
* Divine love as a refining fire
* Restoration through judgment
* Childlikeness and spiritual maturity
* Self-denial and the death of the false self
* Freedom through surrender
* Resurrection and eternal life
* The restoration of God's image in every person

## Source Material

These devotionals are based on **George MacDonald's *Unspoken Sermons***, with each series adapted in sequence while preserving the theological intent and spiritual movement of the original.
