## 100 - The Inheritance of Light

> *In the kingdom of God, nothing truly possessed is diminished by being shared. Every soul who enters the light enlarges the joy of all the rest.*

Earthly inheritance is divided by possession. The more one receives, the less remains for another. But the inheritance of the saints is unlike every treasure we have known. God is not portioned out among His children. The more fully you receive Him, the more of Him you are able to give; and the more others receive, the richer your own life becomes. Every new heart awakened to love increases the gladness of heaven.

Our inheritance is light, and the Light is God Himself. Every beauty that has ever stirred you, every truth that has awakened your mind, every act of mercy that has lifted you beyond yourself, has been some ray of His presence. The flower, the wind, the face of a friend, the courage of the humble, the imagination of a child—each is precious because something of the Father shines through it.

No creature reveals Him in precisely the same way. You carry some word of God that no one else can speak exactly as you do. Every person is a facet through which the infinite Light shines with a different glory. Therefore you cannot receive your inheritance alone. What God gives you belongs also to your brothers and sisters, and what He gives them is part of His gift to you. To withhold your true self from love is to make the whole family poorer.

Heaven is not an endless religious ceremony, nor an escape from the good things God has begun here. It is the fulfilment of them. It is life without the veil: work without bondage, beauty without decay, companionship without fear, repentance without hiding, and love without the shadow of loss. There we shall still discover, create, serve, reconcile, and grow. Every throb of life will say God, because nothing will remain separated from its source.

And there we shall know one another more truly, not less. We will speak the apologies that pride once silenced and receive the forgiveness for which our hearts have longed. Love will not be erased but perfected. The Father who gave us to one another did not create affection to mock it. He is preparing a kingdom where every true bond may become open, free, and everlasting.

Do not be afraid that heaven will make you less yourself. Your freedom will be complete when nothing in you moves apart from the Father. As a stream is most fully itself while it remains joined to its spring, so you will become most truly yourself when your will moves in harmony with His. Your inheritance is not merely a place awaiting you. It is the life of God awakening within you even now.

God is, and all is well. What we have seen is only the beginning. Every good thing is a promise, every beauty an earnest, every act of love a doorway. Live, then, in expectation. The Father has not exhausted His imagination, and the children of light have scarcely begun to enter their inheritance.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, awaken me to the inheritance of Your light.*
