## 98 - Nothing Hidden

> *God brings all things into the light, not because He delights in exposure, but because He intends to make us true. The final unveiling is the mercy by which every mask is removed and every child is set free.*

God is not hiding truth from His children. Whatever remains dark to us is dark only because we are not yet ready to see it. His whole work is revelation: showing us Himself, showing us one another, and showing us our own hearts, so that we may come at last into the freedom of those who have nothing to conceal.

This is why hypocrisy cannot endure. To hide what we are while trying to appear better is to build a house without windows and then call it light. The Father will not leave us imprisoned there. He desires that we should live beneath the open heavens, with no thought we are unwilling for Him to see and no secret life we must protect from those who love us.

Yet the promise that all will be revealed is also comfort for the misunderstood. If you have tried to obey Christ and have been judged falsely, you need not spend your life defending yourself. The truth does not depend upon the opinions of others. It waits in God, and in His time every fog will lift. You are free to do what is right and leave your reputation in the Father’s keeping.

But do not ask for the unveiling of others unless you are willing to be unveiled yourself. Perhaps the judgment you long to see corrected will reveal that you too were mistaken. Perhaps another’s motives were better than you imagined, or your own less pure. The lover of truth welcomes even the discovery that humbles him, for he would rather lose his innocence in his own eyes than keep a lie.

The Father will be perfectly fair with you. He will not expose what He has already healed as though it still defined you, nor conceal what must be seen for your freedom. Whatever shame He allows will be cleansing, never cruel. To bow before the truth is not destruction. It is the moment the false self falls away and the child God has always known begins to stand in the light.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, make me willing to be fully known.*
