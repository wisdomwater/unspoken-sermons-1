## 97 - Made Righteous

> *Righteousness is not a rule kept at a distance, but the life of God taking root within us. The Father does not merely command us to become good; He gives Himself until goodness becomes our joy.*

The righteousness that comes from God is not a borrowed garment laid over an unchanged heart. It is the very character of God growing within His child through trust and obedience. To become righteous is to learn to see as the Father sees, to love what He loves, and to desire nothing that would injure another or separate us from His life.

No collection of laws, however perfectly kept, could by itself make a person righteous. True righteousness reaches deeper than conduct. It is a heart so awake to love that injustice becomes unbearable, not merely because it is forbidden, but because another person is precious. It is the glad fulfilment of our relationship with God and with every brother and sister He has given us.

The righteous soul does not continually ask, “What am I allowed to do?” It asks, “What is true? What is loving? What belongs to the Father?” Such a person is slowly freed from anxious self-concern. If God desires something for him, he knows it will come in its proper hour. If God does not desire it, he learns not to cling to it. His joy no longer rises from possessing his own life, but from sharing the life of God with others.

How will the Father accomplish this in you? You may not yet understand. The clay does not know every movement of the potter’s hand, nor does the stone comprehend the finished form hidden in the sculptor’s thought. Your part is not to master the mystery but to remain willing beneath His hands. Obey the light you have. Open the door when He knocks. Go out to meet Him when He calls you beyond the familiar rooms of yourself.

Do not imagine that divine service is confined to sacred places. The world in which you work, speak, forgive, endure, and love is the Father’s temple. Every neighbour is an altar upon which love may be offered. To serve another without seeking praise is to serve Christ. Let others misunderstand you if they must; only take care that you do not turn your heart away from them. The Father is making you righteous wherever love is chosen.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, form Your righteousness within me.*
