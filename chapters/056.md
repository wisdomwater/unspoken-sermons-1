## 56 - More Life Still

> *God does not give life sparingly. His whole heart is poured out that His children might share His own life without measure.*

Everything Christ did reveals what the Father has always been doing. The cross did not awaken love in God; it unveiled the love that has always burned in His heart. From the beginning, the Father has given Himself for His creation. He does not stand apart from our suffering, watching from a distance. He enters into it, bears it, and patiently labors until His children are made fully alive. His sacrifice is nothing less than the continual self-giving of perfect love.

If His work seems slow, it is because love refuses to settle for less than our true good. We often ask for relief when the Father desires life. We long for escape, while He longs to heal us from the roots outward. Every disappointment, every holy dissatisfaction, every ache that reminds us we are not yet whole becomes another invitation to receive more of His life. He never withholds Himself; it is we who are slowly learning how deeply we may receive Him.

Whenever you grow weary, remember that it is not life that has become your enemy. It is the shadow of death—the fear, exhaustion, distrust, and falsehood that cling to life like winter clings to the earth before spring. The answer is never less life, but more. Ask not merely for comfort, but for a fuller share in the living presence of God. His strength does not despise weakness; it quietly enters it until weakness itself becomes the place where His life is most clearly revealed.

So when your heart is tired, do not surrender to despair. Lift even the smallest desire toward the Father. If all you can say is, "I would yet live; I would yet trust; I would yet love," then His life is already stirring within you. The One who first breathed life into your soul has not ceased His work. He will continue enlarging your heart until joy becomes as natural as breathing and love becomes the very atmosphere in which you live.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, fill me with more of Your life today.*
