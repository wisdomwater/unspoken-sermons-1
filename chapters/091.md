## 91 - God Is Light

> *The best news the world has ever received is not merely that God saves, but that God is altogether good. There is no shadow in Him, only light strong enough to heal every darkness.*

When John searched for words to describe all that he had learned from walking with Jesus, he did not begin with doctrines or explanations. He simply declared, "God is light." This is the heart of the gospel. Christ did not come to persuade us to trust a God whose goodness is uncertain. He came to show us the Father exactly as He is. Everything Jesus said, every hand He stretched out, every tear He shed, every enemy He forgave, revealed the same unchanging truth: there is no darkness in the heart of God.

Because God is light, you need never fear that perfect love hides some secret cruelty. Whatever is truly good, pure, beautiful, and just finds its source in Him. There may be much that I cannot yet understand, for His light is brighter than my eyes can bear, but mystery is not the same as darkness. What I cannot yet see clearly will one day shine with a beauty that causes wonder instead of fear. The Father is never less good than Christ has shown Him to be.

Do not be afraid, then, to bring every honest question into His presence. He has not asked you to silence the deepest witness of conscience, but to let it be purified until it sees more clearly. If something appears unlike the love revealed in Jesus, wait patiently before the Light. He is able to correct your sight without asking you to distrust His character. A child may misunderstand the Father's ways, but the Father never ceases to be good.

And because God is light, He will not leave you in darkness. He loves you too well to abandon you to the shadows that wound and deceive. Whatever in you belongs to His life He will strengthen; whatever belongs to fear, pride, or falsehood He will gently burn away. His judgments are not the blows of an offended ruler but the healing work of a loving Father determined to restore His child.

So come to Him exactly as you are. Bring your hopes and your failures, your longing for holiness and your weariness of trying, your unanswered questions and your hidden shame. Hide nothing from the One who is light. He knows how to separate truth from falsehood, life from death, and the child He created from all that has burdened that child. His light does not expose you to condemn you. It shines so that you may finally become free.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, lead me into Your healing light.*
