## 15 - The Secret Name

> *The Father does not make copies. Every soul is a unique thought of His love, and each is called to reveal something of Him that no other can.*

Your true name is not something you invent, nor is it something the world assigns to you. It is the name the Father has always known. Before you had become the person He created you to be, He already held your completed life within His heart, just as the mighty oak is hidden within the acorn. Yet the name can only be received when the life has grown into its fullness. We must become what God has always seen before we can understand what He has always called us.

This is why our journey with God is never merely about escaping sin. It is about becoming ourselves. Every act of surrender, every quiet victory over pride, every faithful step toward love brings us nearer to the person the Father envisioned from the beginning. When at last He speaks the new name, it will not be a surprise to Him. It will be His joyful declaration that His good work has come to its beautiful completion.

No one else can receive your name because no one else can stand before God exactly as you do. Each soul bears a distinct likeness of the infinite beauty of the Father. One heart reflects His tenderness, another His courage, another His joy, another His wisdom. We need one another because no single life can reveal the whole glory of God. Every redeemed person becomes a living word spoken by the Father into His creation.

There is, then, a holy meeting place between your soul and God that belongs to no one else. It is there that He teaches you who He is in a way no other person can fully know. And what you discover in that secret place is not meant to remain hidden forever. As a flower opens simply by becoming what it was created to be, so your life becomes a revelation of the Father to others. The truest worship is not to imitate another saint, but to become the unique child through whom God's own love may shine.

**Breath Prayer**

> *Father, help me become the child You have always known.*
